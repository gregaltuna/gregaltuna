Freeman Online Mobile Delivers:

  • Show Information

    The information you need just a tap away. Access shipping addresses, facility rules and with our integrated calendar feature, add all important show dates to your calendar with ease.

  • Shop

    An intuitive ordering and check-out experience puts the right products and services at your fingertips. With intuitive search and a mobile first design it’s easy to order those last minute items to make your booth complete.

  • Freight Services

    Through our multi-function Freight Services feature, track your shipments every step of the way with ease. Access to key shipping details means you can stop worrying about your freight.

  • Concierge Services

    Get assistance through Concierge Services no matter what you need. Whether you are submitting a real-time request, calling or emailing, all via the app – our customer service experts are ready to help.

  • Notifications

    Freight and electrical notifications keep you informed of progress along the way.

  • Move-Out

    We make checking out of the show as simple as 1-2-3. Now view, update and submit your MHA right from the app to help get you on your way after a busy show.

  • Order History

    With order history, quickly reference a summary of all orders placed pre-show, invoices from previous events and keep all important show records in one organized location.
