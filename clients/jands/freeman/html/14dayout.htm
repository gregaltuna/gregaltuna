We are excited to introduce you to the beta version of Freeman Online Mobile, an app that provides you with all the right tools to ensure a seamless execution at show site, from move-in to move-out.

With the new mobile app you will be able to:

  • Access key show information for all of your shows
  • Place orders for products and services at show site
  • Track Freeman freight shipments
  • Submit service requests with our new Concierge Services feature
  • Receive important notifications to keep you informed
  • Quickly submit move-out paperwork
  • Access orders placed for your event

Please select “Learn More” to be taken to the Freeman Online Mobile App page to download the app.

After your show, we will be sending out a survey to gather your feedback on the app and to learn how we can make your show site experience even better.

Thanks in advance for participating!
